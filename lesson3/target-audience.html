Target Audience

Audience Description

Persons with of income types located within Idaho and within a 50 mile radius of an Idaho border. Target age of 18 to 40 years old. Experience with technology is expected to be relatively high. Technical skill with technology is not required beyond a simple understanding on how to use the Internet. Most users will be mobile based.

Persona One » Penelope

Age: 28 years old

Income: $35k a year - Medical Receptionist

Relevant Identity Information: Spends much of her time inside working, playing with jigsaw puzzles and bridge, as well as going to the movies as often as she can. Her most pressing concern regarding the weather include driving conditions and what effect the weather will have on her son’s playtime.

Family Description: Single with a child named Danny (age 10) from a previous relationship. Never has married. Her mother died when she was a preteen and she was raised by her father. Her previous relationship ended when she felt like her ex was treating both her son and her poorly and not taking care of their needs.

Persona Two » Yasin

Age: 20 years old

Income: N/A - Student

Relevant Identity Information: An athletic student majoring in food science, most of his concerns are with outdoor activities. As a swimmer and dog enthusiast, information regarding precipitation and temperature are the most important to him.

Family Description: Today is the day that a dog show event will take place. Yasin was thinking about going but hasn’t planned-ahead. The event will start in an hour. He has been stuck in classes and can’t see outside. He goes to myidahoweather.net on his phone to quickly see what the weather is currently looking like.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: After hearing about a school trip that will take place on a workday, Penelope needs to know what kind of clothing she will need to give to Danny that day. The trip is scheduled four days from now. Penelope also wants to know what the driving conditions will be as she drives to Danny’s school and work that day. She goes to myidahoweather.net using her laptop to check the latest forecast.

Scenario 2: Today is the day that a dog show event will take place. Yasin was thinking about going but hasn’t planned-ahead. The event will start in an hour. He has been stuck in classes and can’t see outside. He goes to myidahoweather.net on his phone to quickly see what the weather is currently looking like.

Scenario 3: Penelope now has a day to herself. She is feeling a little bit like she has spent too much time inside and needs to go out to enjoy herself. After seeing advertisements for a local dog show, she checks with the current conditions to see if her allergies are going to be an issue.

Scenario 4: Finally out of class and finally heading over on the Idaho Falls bus to the dog show, Yasin takes out his phone to see what the weather will be like in just an hour. He is pleased to find that the weather is going to be perfect during the show.

Scenario 5: During the show, our lucky two people end up meeting and hitting it off. Yasin upon finding out that they both have free time tonight, asks Penelope out. She asks what they should do. Yasin pulls out his phone to check the weather for that night to see if his favorite outdoor restaurant would be a good idea. Of course Penelope notices that he uses the same amazing website and is impressed with his intellect in using such a smart tool.
